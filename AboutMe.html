About Me

Hi! My name is Sam Shenoi and I am currently a student at Baylor University majoring in bioinformatics on the premed track. I'm really interested in the combination of medicine and computer science especially as it relates to bio and clinical informatic systems.



As a computer scientist, I'm currently working on app development for two different projects. The first project involves the creation of an Android App for researchers at a local Health center in the Waco area. The second project invovles the creation of an Android Wear App to help ADHD kids pay attention in class.



As a biologist, I currently serve on the editorial board of Baylor University's undergraduate research journal, Scientia.
